Cookie Policy

Last Updated: April 7, 2026

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how MCPSuperHero uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website and service. We are committed to being transparent about our data collection practices.

2. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow websites to recognize your device and remember information about your interactions with the site. Cookies can be "persistent" (remaining on your device until you delete them) or "session" (deleted when you close your browser).

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the basic functionality of our website and service. Without these cookies, you would not be able to log in or use core features.

  • Session identification cookies
  • Authentication and security cookies
  • User preference cookies

Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website, allowing us to identify errors and improve performance.

  • Google Analytics cookies (tracking website traffic and user behavior)
  • Performance monitoring cookies

Functional Cookies

These cookies remember your choices and preferences to provide a personalized experience.

  • Theme preference cookies
  • Language preference cookies
  • Navigation preference cookies

Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to track your interests and show relevant advertisements. You can opt out of these cookies.

  • Google Tag Manager cookies
  • Conversion tracking cookies
  • Advertising network cookies

4. Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party services that place cookies on your device:

  • Google Analytics: Helps us analyze website traffic and user behavior. Cookies are identified by names like _ga and _gid.
  • Google Tag Manager: Manages tags and tracking pixels for conversion tracking and analytics.
  • Stripe: Processes payments and may set cookies for payment processing.

5. Cookie Duration

Session Cookies: Automatically deleted when you close your browser

Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for 1 year to 2 years, or until manually deleted

Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics cookies typically expire after 2 years of inactivity

6. How to Control Cookies

You have several options to control cookies:

Browser Controls

Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. You can typically control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Cookies and other site data

Opt-Out Tools

You can opt out of specific tracking services:

  • Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on
  • Digital Advertising Alliance Opt-Out

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding cookies and tracking:

  • GDPR (EU/EEA): You have the right to consent to non-essential cookies before they are set.
  • CCPA (California): You have the right to opt-out of the sale of personal information.
  • Other Jurisdictions: You may have similar rights to control your data.

8. Tracking Technologies

In addition to cookies, we may use other tracking technologies:

  • Pixels (Web Beacons): Small, transparent images that track page views and conversions
  • Local Storage: Browser storage for user preferences and app data
  • Log Files: Server logs that record IP addresses and access information

9. Do Not Track

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" feature. MCPSuperHero respects this preference and does not engage in tracking or targeted advertising for users with Do Not Track enabled in their browser settings.

10. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and posting the revised policy on our website.
